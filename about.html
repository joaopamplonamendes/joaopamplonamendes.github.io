About

João Pamplona Mendes is a filmmaker based in Lisbon, Portugal. He works as a Director, Editor, Assistant Director, Music Composer and Sound Designer. He wrote and directed the short films "Além o Tejo" in 2021, “Pinhas” in 2023 and “Geração” in 2025, the videoclips “Pai” in 2022 and “Surto” in 2025 and directed in partnership with Alexandre Cavadas the short films "Abraçar o báltico" in 2022 and “Pedais” in 2024. He also composed and recorded the music for the poetry album "A Casa". He worked as an assistant director for films such as Sunset: The Mystery of the Necklace of São Cajó (2023), The Bloodless Revolution (2024), Turn of the Tide: Seasons 2 (2025) and 3 (Unreleased), and he regularly sound designs and composes soundtracks for animated videos, collaborating with Aim Creative Studios for clients such as TED Education. In parallel, he has a master's degree in Industrial Engineering and Management at Instituto Superior Técnico, Lisbon.
